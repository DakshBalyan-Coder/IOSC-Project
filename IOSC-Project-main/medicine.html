Paracetamol

Active Ingredient: Paracetamol

Dosage: The usual adult dosage for Paracetamol is 500mg to 1000mg every 4 to 6 hours as needed, with a maximum of 4g (4000mg) per day. Dosages for children depend on their weight and age and should be determined by a healthcare professional.

Formulation: Paracetamol is available in various forms, including tablets, capsules, oral suspension, and effervescent tablets.

Warnings: Do not exceed the recommended dosage to avoid liver damage. It's important to check other medications for Paracetamol content to avoid accidental overdose. Individuals with liver disease or a history of alcohol abuse should consult a healthcare professional before taking Paracetamol. Long-term or excessive use may lead to liver damage. Allergic reactions such as skin rash or difficulty breathing may occur. If any adverse reactions occur, discontinue use and seek medical attention.

Ostrocalcium Cough Syrup

Active Ingredient: Ostrocalcium cough syrup typically contains a combination of active ingredients such as: Dextromethorphan (cough suppressant) Guaifenesin (expectorant) Chlorpheniramine (antihistamine)

Dosage: Dosage may vary depending on the specific formulation and the patient's age and condition. It's crucial to follow the instructions provided on the product label or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Formulation: Ostrocalcium cough syrup is typically available in liquid form, usually in bottles with a measuring cup or spoon for accurate dosage.

Warnings: Avoid using other cough, cold, or allergy medications while taking Ostrocalcium cough syrup without consulting a healthcare professional, as it may lead to an overdose of certain ingredients. This medication may cause drowsiness, so it's important to avoid activities requiring alertness, such as driving or operating machinery, until you know how the medication affects you. Consult a healthcare professional before using this medication if you have certain medical conditions such as high blood pressure, heart disease, or thyroid problems. Avoid alcohol while taking Ostrocalcium cough syrup, as it may increase the risk of side effects such as drowsiness or dizziness. If symptoms persist or worsen despite treatment, seek medical advice promptly.
